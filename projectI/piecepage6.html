The Face of Genius, 1926

The Face of Genius, painted in 1926, is one of Magrittes earlier works, and definitely very different from what other artists in that era would've usually painted. This image depicts a white face with two cube faced holes, on a wooden plank, also with cubic holes and dark trees to the front and back of the face, with a dark, foggy background. This image invokes mystery and thoughts of self assessment.
