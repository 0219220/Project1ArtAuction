The Evening Gown, 1954

The Evening Gown, painted with oils on canvas by Magritte in 1954, has a beautiful composition of the bare back of a woman, standing in what seems to be an endless body of water under a shining, crescent new moon. In a letter addressed to a friend of his, he states in remembrance on how he came about this image and writes about how the positioning of elements, like this one, can give paintings new, magnificent compositions.
