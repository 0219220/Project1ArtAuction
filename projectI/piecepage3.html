Black Magic, 1934

Black Magic, painted by Magritte with oils on linen in 1934, depicts a woman in front of a large body of water and a cloud filled sky meeting while the figure of a beautiful woman, grasping with her right hand a rock, is slowly blending into the background from top to bottom. Magritte clearly intends to remind us that we as human beings, are one with nature and that we are attached to Earth.
